Parallax Scrolling Effects

Header Section: The company's logo and navigation menu stay fixed at the top of the page, while the background image of a mountain range slowly scrolls upward. This creates a sense of depth and draws the user's attention to the stunning scenery. Hero Section: A large image of hikers on a trail comes into view. As the user scrolls, the hikers appear to move down the trail, giving the impression of progression and adventure. Featured Destinations: The next section showcases featured hiking destinations. Each destination's image is set to move at a slightly slower speed than the user's scroll, creating a parallax effect that makes the images feel like they're floating in the foreground. Testimonials: Customer testimonials are displayed in a section where the background is a tranquil forest. As the user scrolls, the forest background subtly shifts, adding a sense of tranquility and authenticity to the testimonials. Call to Action: The final section encourages users to book a hiking adventure. Here, the background shows a campsite under the stars. As the user scrolls, the stars twinkle and move slightly, creating a magical atmosphere..
